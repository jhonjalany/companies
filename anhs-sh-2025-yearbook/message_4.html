MESSAGE

        "The youth of today are the leaders of tomorrow." – Nelson Mandela

        As we celebrate the achievements of our graduates today, these words by Nelson Mandela feel especially relevant. You, the young people of today, are the ones who will shape the future of our country. With the theme “Henerasyon ng Pagkakaisa: Kaagapay ng Bagong Pilipinas” (Generation of Unity: Partners for the New Philippines), it’s clear that this moment marks the start of a journey toward a brighter and better nation.

        You, the new generation, are in a unique position to become the foundation of progress. You have the power to create a society that values unity, innovation, and compassion. Our country stands at a turning point, and your collective strength will decide the future we build together. The way you unite, rise above divisions, and work together will shape the Philippines for years to come.

        As you move forward, remember that you are not limited by the mistakes of those who came before you. While the wisdom of experience is valuable, it is your energy, creativity, and passion that will drive our nation forward. You’re not simply inheriting the past—you have the power to change it. Strive to do better. Build the connections that will bring us together as a united people, determined to lift one another up.

        In the Bible, 1 Timothy 4:12 reminds us: "Don’t let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, and in purity." This verse calls upon you to lead with integrity, to set the standard for others through your actions, words, and the love you show one another. You are not limited by your age, but empowered by your passion and your capacity for change.

        This journey is not yours alone. To the parents and families, I offer my heartfelt congratulations and thanks. Your unwavering support has played a key role in helping these young minds reach this milestone. To the educators who have worked so hard to guide and shape the future, your efforts have created the foundation for today’s success. The partnership between family, school, and community has been vital in nurturing these future leaders.

        As we celebrate your accomplishments, I urge you to keep in mind that the road ahead won’t always be easy. Building the New Philippines will require resilience, vision, and commitment. But as graduates of this generation, you’ve already proven your strength in overcoming challenges. It’s now in your hands to build a society that’s more inclusive, just, and united. Together, you have the power to create a new and better Philippines.

        Go forward with the knowledge that you are the architects of tomorrow. The future is yours to shape—make it one of unity, progress, and prosperity for all. Congratulations, graduates. May you lead us into a bright new era for our country.

        Long live the Filipino spirit!

EVELYN R. FETALVERO, CESO IV

Regional director
