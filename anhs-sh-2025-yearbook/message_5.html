MESSAGE

        "Opportunities are not merely found; they are created through collaboration." The late Henry Sy Sr., a visionary entrepreneur, understood that success is not a solo journey—it is built on teamwork, shared vision, and collective effort.

        Today, as completers and graduates, you stand at the gateway of limitless possibilities. Your achievements are not just the result of individual perseverance but also of the relationships you’ve built, the ideas you’ve shared, and the unity you’ve fostered.

        Collaboration fuels progress, strengthens resilience, and transforms dreams into reality. In our careers, communities, and personal journeys, working together allows us to overcome challenges and unlock opportunities that once seemed out of reach. As you step forward, embrace the power of unity—because when we lift each other up, we create a future where success is not just personal but shared.

        This year’s theme, "Henerasyon ng Pagkakaisa: Kaagapay sa Bagong Pilipinas" (Generation of Unity: Partners for the New Philippines), reminds us that true progress comes from solidarity. You are not just completers or graduates—you are builders of a better tomorrow. You embody the strength and diversity of our nation, a generation ready to weave together the hopes and aspirations of our people into a brighter, more inclusive future.

        Let your education be your compass, guiding you toward a future where progress is shared, opportunities have no boundaries, and the spirit of Bayanihan remains the heart of our society. Be more than spectators—be partners in change, advocates for unity, and champions of transformation.

        To our dedicated administrators and teachers, thank you for your unwavering commitment and passion, which have shaped and guided every learner.

        To our amazing parents and guardians, your sacrifices and steadfast support have been the foundation of your children's success.

        And to our dear completers and graduates, congratulations! This is not just the end of a chapter, but it is the beginning of a journey filled with new opportunities. Keep striving for excellence, chase your dreams, and always remember that unity is the key to a brighter future.

        Mabuhay kayong lahat, at mabuhay ang Pilipinas!

MARIZA S. MAGAN EdD, CESO V

Schools division superintendent
