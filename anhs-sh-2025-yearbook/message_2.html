OATH AND PLEDGE

ANHS ALUMNI OATH

        We, the 2025 Graduates of Abuyog National High School being solemnly sworn – in today, as members of the Abuyog National High School Alumni Association, do solemnly, earnestly, and sincerely swear, that we will do our best, to fulfill our duties and obligations, to uphold its ideas and to promote its objective and to exercise all our responsibilities, without mental reservations or purpose of evasion.

        So help me God!!!

PLEDGE OF LOYALTY

        Dear Alma Mater, we are fully aware that upon leaving your portals, we are to face the different challenges that threaten the destruction of mankind. We do hereby, pledge that whatever field of specialization each of us has opted, we will strive to become desirable, useful, responsible and versatile citizens of the country, a working entity to work for a cause to achieve a better Philippines. A herculean task of nation building it may be, Juan Edgardo m. angara we commit to join forces for it’s success.

        Intellectual we are, we are sure to practice the good values you have painstakingly inculcated in us, productive management of time, efforts and energy, love and faith in God.

        Walking with Him, we are to uphold with dignity your good name, the Abuyog National High School, and to live the fullest in the service of mankind. Rest assured your efforts in nurturing us will not in vain.

        So help me God!!!
